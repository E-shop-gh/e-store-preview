Data Safety Instructions

  • Choose a strong password - at least 12 characters with mix of letters, numbers and symbols
  • Store passwords securely - you'll need the same password to import data
  • Backup regularly - export data at the end of each business day
  • Verify imports - check data after import to ensure everything transferred correctly
  • Keep multiple backups - maintain backups from different dates
